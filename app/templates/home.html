學校簡介 Introduction

哥城中文學校 - 位於俄亥俄州哥倫布市，是一個非營利的中文及華人文化學校。本校提供中班(Pre-k)到高三(12年級)的課程、成人會話課程、以及許多中文第二外語課程。
Columbus Chinese Academy, a non-profit Chinese language and culture school located at Columbus Ohio. This Chinese School offers Chinese classes to pre-k to 12th grade, Chinese Adult Conversation, and several CFL (Chinese as Foreign Language) classes.
