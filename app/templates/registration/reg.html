Enrollment & Registration

Note 1:

New Family refers to a family that never had any members enrolled in CCA. If you or your kids have registered previously, please go to Existing Student Registration.

Note 2:

If your family is an existing family and would like to enroll new student(s), please email officer.cca@gmail.com to retrieve a new student ID and DO NOT register as a new family. Our FamilyID and StudentID table is based on the enrollment in the previous semester, so it is possible that you cannot find your IDs. In this case, please also email us and DO NOT register as a new family.

CCA New Family Enrollment:


  1. Complete your family and student enrollment at CCA New Family Enrollment.
  2. Retrieve your Family ID and Student ID at the CCA registration desk on site.
  3. Follow the instructions of CCA Existing Student Registration below.

CCA Existing Student Registration:


  1. Check your Family ID & Student ID at FamilyID & StudentID Table.
  2. Register EACH student at CCA Existing Student Registration. (Be sure to click Submit to complete registration)
  3. Calculate total tuition at CCA Tuition Calculator.
  4. Bring a check with your Family ID noted on the check. (If paying cash, please inform CCA officers your Family ID.)

Note: There is no need to print the registration form.
